Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

1. Introduction

NexusOffice ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our social media management application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you connect your Facebook, Instagram, or Twitter accounts, we access basic profile information.
  • Social Media Content: Posts, comments, messages, and media you create or interact with through our platform.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you use NexusOffice, including features accessed and actions performed.
  • Authentication Tokens: OAuth tokens to access your social media accounts on your behalf.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Manage and publish content to your connected social media accounts
  • Read and respond to comments and messages on your behalf
  • Provide analytics and insights about your social media performance
  • Improve and optimize our AI-powered features
  • Provide customer support

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All authentication tokens are encrypted and stored securely
  • Data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Access to your data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We regularly review and update our security practices

5. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • With your explicit consent

6. Third-Party Services

NexusOffice integrates with the following third-party platforms:

  • Meta (Facebook/Instagram): Subject to Meta Privacy Policy
  • Twitter/X: Subject to Twitter Privacy Policy
  • LinkedIn: Subject to LinkedIn Privacy Policy

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke access to your social media accounts at any time
  • Export your data in a portable format

8. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. When you disconnect a social media account or delete your NexusOffice account, we delete associated data within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

NexusOffice is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
